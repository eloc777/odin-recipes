Chilli con kang

Ingredients

  • 500g Kangaroo mince
  • 1 Capscicum diced
  • 1 Brown onion diced
  • 3 Cloves garlic crushed
  • 300g Kale chopped
  • 1 Can diced tomatoes
  • 1 Tbsp Paprika
  • 1 Tbsp Cumin
  • 1 Tbsp Salt
  • 1 Tbsp Cayenne pepper

Method

  1. Saute onion and garlic on medium heat until onion is translucent (2min)
  2. Add mince and cook until browned
  3. Add seasoning
  4. Add remaining vegetable
  5. Cook further 5 min or until desired
