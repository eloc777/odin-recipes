Spanish Omlette

As you can see, this is a beautiful spanish omlette

Ingredients

  • 2 Potatoes evenly diced
  • 1 Red onion diced
  • 1 Spring onion diced
  • 4 Eggs
  • 100g Cheese
  • 1 Tsp Salt

Method

  1. Fry onions and potatoes in healthy amount of oil
  2. Remove once potatoes soft and crispy
  3. Whisk eggs and add to pan
  4. Return potaoes and onion
  5. Loosen omlette from pan and flip onto a plate
  6. Return omlette to the pan facing the opposite way
  7. Add cheese
  8. Remove once cooked to preference
